## 📋 What is POLYMARKET-AI-TRADING-BOT?

POLYMARKET-AI-TRADING-BOT is a program designed to help you trade automatically on Polymarket. It uses smart algorithms to analyze markets and decide when to buy or sell. This lets you participate in trading without needing to watch the markets all the time.

This app targets traders who want to use AI-driven strategies like arbitrage and copy trading. The bot acts on your behalf and can help you make trading decisions faster than manual methods.

## 🔧 Configuration and Setup

After opening the bot for the first time, you need to configure your trading account.

### Step 1: Connect Your Polymarket Account

Enter your Polymarket API key when prompted. This key lets the bot access your trading account safely. You can get your API key within your Polymarket user settings.

### Step 2: Adjust Trading Preferences

Set how much money you want the bot to trade with each time. You can also define limits for risk and maximum daily trades. Defaults are set to moderate levels, which are good for new users.

### Step 3: Choose Trading Strategy

Select from available strategies like:

- Arbitrage: Capture price differences between markets.
- Copy Trading: Follow trades from successful traders automatically.
- AI Prediction: Use machine learning models to anticipate market moves.

You can change these settings anytime in the bot’s preferences menu.
